Project Medic-Innovate.AI

Early diagnostic on CT-scan of COVID-19 than RT-PCR COVID-19 test using machine learning to assist medical staff for optimal COVID-19 therapy. Applying Artificial Intelligence on COVID-19 diagnostic using:

  • X-Ray images
  • CT-Scan images
  • Patient's clinical data

This research is not only used in diagnosing but also in determining disease staging, based on clinical, laboratory, and radiological symptoms found in chest X-Rays and CT-Scans. Determining disease staging can be used as a reference in disease assessment to be used in the determination of disease mortality.
